This is Benjamin Banneker

Born on November 09, 1731 in Baltimore County, MD, Banneker was a free African-American. Later on in his life, he was known for his advanced studies in mathematics, astrology, and engineering.

Interesting Facts

Some interesting facts about Benjamin Banneker are:
- He was an African-American Scientist and this meant that he was often disgraced and thought of poorly
- He was never taught or went through proper studies
- He was a farmer for his entire life
- He made a prediction about the 1789 Solar Eclipse
- He got the opportunity to survey the ground for the US Capitol
- His fame was mostly derived from his ALMANACS
- He died EXACTLY one month before his 75th birthday
- There was a postage stamp created after him

Banneker's Family

Benjamin Banneker was raised by both parents and ended up having THREE sisters. Though he never married or had children.
